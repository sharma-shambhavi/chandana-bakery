Our Sweet Story

Baking happiness since 2010

From a Small Oven to Shahjahanpur's Favorite

Founded in 2010 by Mrs. Chandana Sharma, our bakery began as a humble home kitchen with just one oven and a dream. What started as a passion for creating delicious treats for family and friends quickly grew into a beloved neighborhood institution.

Today, we still use Mrs. Sharma's original recipes, mixing traditional techniques with high-quality local ingredients. Every cake, pastry, and loaf of bread is made with the same love and care as those first batches from our founder's kitchen.

We're proud to serve Shahjahanpur's sweetest moments - from birthday celebrations to wedding anniversaries, and everything in between.

Our Baking Philosophy

What makes our treats special

Quality Ingredients

We source fresh, local ingredients and never use artificial preservatives. Our flour comes from local mills, and we use real butter in every recipe.

Handcrafted With Love

Every item is made by hand in small batches. Our bakers start work at 4 AM daily to ensure you get the freshest products possible.

Traditional Recipes

We preserve authentic baking methods passed down through generations. Our signature jar cakes use a 50-year-old family recipe.
